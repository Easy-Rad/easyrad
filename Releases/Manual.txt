First unset all the pre-existing keys on the dictaphone.

Buttons:
- Transcribe: focus on PowerScribe (single click), view report (double click)
- Prev: same
- Next: same
- Play/Stop: toggle zoom/3d cursor
- Backward: delete when focused on PowerScribe, previous series on IV
- Forward: open new line when focused on PowerScribe, next series on IV
- Left button: open search and paste
- OK button: reset viewport
- Right button: open Comrad and copy
